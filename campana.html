La Campana Café
A Closer Look

Exuding a rustic charm reminiscent of the Spanish era, La Campana Café and Souvenir Shop in Dupax del Sur offers guests a refreshing outdoor experience. With its open-air setting, traditional touches, and laid-back atmosphere, the café invites visitors to slow down and savor a cup of local coffee or enjoy light snacks under a breezy, heritage-inspired ambiance. Complementing the café is a souvenir shop that showcases locally crafted products, delicacies, and keepsakes, perfect for those who wish to bring home a piece of Dupax del Sur’s rich culture and history.
