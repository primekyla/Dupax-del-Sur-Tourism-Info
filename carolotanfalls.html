Carolotan Falls
A Closer Look

Carolotan Falls is a natural wonder tucked within the lush landscapes of Dupax del Sur. Cascading gracefully into a cool basin, the falls offer a refreshing retreat for nature lovers and adventurers alike. Surrounded by verdant forests and tranquil scenery, it provides the perfect setting for swimming, picnics, and quiet relaxation. Its unspoiled beauty and soothing waters make Carolotan Falls not just a tourist spot, but also a peaceful escape where visitors can reconnect with nature.
