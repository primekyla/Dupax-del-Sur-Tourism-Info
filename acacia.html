Century Old Acacia Tree
A Closer Look

Located at Brgy. Dopaj, Dupax del Sur, the century-old Acacia tree stands as a living witness to the town’s rich history and cultural heritage. With its sprawling branches and massive trunk, this natural landmark has provided shade and shelter to generations of Dupajeños. More than just a tree, it symbolizes strength, resilience, and continuity, linking the past to the present. Its majestic canopy has long been a gathering place for locals, making it a silent keeper of stories, traditions, and community life.
