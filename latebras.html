AG Latebras Campground
A Closer Look

AG Latebras Campground in Sitio Abuang, Banila, Dupax del Sur, Nueva Vizcaya is a serene nature escape that offers wide open grounds for camping and overlanding, native cottages for comfort, swimming spots for relaxation, and clean facilities for convenience, making it an ideal destination for families, groups, and adventurers who want to disconnect from the city, breathe fresh air, and experience the simple joys of outdoor living surrounded by lush greenery and starry night skies.
